Find your path at LMI

Build mission-critical technology that ships.

At LMI, engineers, data scientists, architects, and technologists solve complex government challenges with modern tools and disciplined delivery. From AI-enabled platforms to secure cloud systems, our teams build deployable solutions designed for real operational environments.

You won’t just prototype, you’ll move capabilities from concept to production, delivering technology that supports national security, operational readiness, and informed decision-making.

You’ll thrive here if you:

  • Want to solve hard technical problems with real-world stakes
  • Value modern engineering practices and tools
  • Thrive in high-trust teams that value autonomy
  • Want to see your work fielded and scaled

Lead complex missions. Deliver measurable outcomes.

Our program managers and mission leaders guide high-impact initiatives that modernize systems, accelerate capability delivery, and strengthen government operations. You’ll coordinate cross-functional teams, manage risk, and ensure technology translates into operational value.

At LMI, leadership means execution—balancing innovation with accountability, speed with security, and ambition with discipline.

You’ll thrive here if you:

  • Excel at navigating complexity
  • Lead diverse technical teams
  • Deliver results in high-stakes environments
  • Build trusted partnerships with government stakeholders

Turn insight into action.

Our analysts and domain experts tackle some of the nation’s most complex operational and strategic challenges. Using data, modeling, and deep subject-matter expertise, you’ll help agencies modernize processes, optimize systems, and make informed decisions that shape outcomes.

From logistics and operations research to policy and systems analysis, your work will directly support mission execution and long-term transformation.

You’ll thrive here if you:

  • Enjoy solving ambiguous problems
  • Translate complex data into clear recommendations
  • Partner closely with operators and decision-makers
  • Value rigor, clarity, and measurable impact

Engineer solutions for complex, real-world environments.

Our systems engineers, digital engineers, and technical specialists design, integrate, and validate capabilities that perform where it matters most. You’ll work across hardware, software, modeling, and mission systems to ensure reliability, interoperability, and operational effectiveness.

We combine technical depth with mission understanding, so what you design works in practice.

You’ll thrive here if you:

  • Think in systems
  • Care about precision and performance
  • Enjoy integrating across disciplines
  • Want to see your designs fielded and sustained

Enable the mission behind the mission.

Our corporate teams power everything we do, from finance and contracts to HR, marketing, and recruiting. You’ll help build the systems, processes, and culture that allow our technical and mission teams to succeed.

At LMI, corporate professionals are strategic partners, ensuring agility, accountability, and operational excellence across the enterprise.

You’ll thrive here if you:

  • Like building scalable processes
  • Enjoy cross-functional collaboration
  • Value precision and accountability
  • Want to contribute to mission impact in a different way

Start your career solving meaningful problems.

Whether you’re a student, recent graduate, or transitioning professional, LMI offers opportunities to work on impactful projects alongside experienced leaders and technologists. You’ll gain real responsibility, hands-on experience, and mentorship from day one.

We invest in developing talent because the next generation of mission leaders starts here.

You’ll thrive here if you:

  • Are curious and driven
  • Want early exposure to meaningful work
  • Value mentorship and growth
  • Are excited to contribute to national impact

Life at LMI

At LMI, we build mission-critical solutions that serve our nation, and that starts with how we work together. We foster a high-trust, collaborative environment where technologists, analysts, engineers, and mission leaders tackle complex challenges as one team. Here, your work connects directly to outcomes that matter.

Values

Our values guide how we build, lead, and deliver. Leadership, mission focus, and innovation define who we are, anchored by ethics and integrity in everything we do.

  • Leadership:  Leadership at LMI is defined by ownership. Every employee is empowered to take initiative, solve problems, and drive progress. We step forward, take responsibility, and act with long-term impact in mind.
  • Mission focus: We exist to strengthen the missions that safeguard and advance our nation. That means understanding our customers’ real challenges and delivering solutions that perform in operational environments.
  • Innovation: Innovation at LMI is disciplined and outcome-driven. We combine emerging technologies with deep domain expertise to modernize systems, accelerate decision-making, and deliver capability at the speed the mission demands.

Graduate Fellowship Program

We invite new graduates with master’s degrees to polish their skills and put their knowledge into action through this 1-year program, after which fellows continue as consultants.

The program supports recent graduate students with exceptional technical and research skills as well as high leadership potential. We seek broad educational and professional experience indicating strong, flexible intellectual capability. We encourage graduate students from business, public administration, data science, engineering, and health policy disciplines to apply.

In addition to the standard employee benefits package, fellows augment their work experience with professional development training. LMI staff members and outside experts supply this mix of project management, professional skills, leadership, and technical instruction. We adapt the training to the interests of each fellow and the needs of LMI.

Each fellow teams with a senior LMI professional for mentorship. Mentors help the fellows prepare professional development and training plans and select projects. The mentors tutor fellows on consulting skills and offer insight and advice on working with the federal government and public sector.

The application process for the Graduate Fellowship Program begins in the early fall of each year. Contact Careers at LMI apply.

Recruiting-Related Scam

LMI is aware that there is a cyber-scam involving false recruiting-related emails directing prospective candidates to provide information via email. Please remain vigilant and ensure you are accessing only our official LMI website, via ICIMS career portal: https://www.lmisolutions.com/careers OR https://careers-lmi.icims.com/jobs/intro

If you would like to confirm if a message you received is legitimate, please email us at recruiting@lmisolutions.com.
